How I work

Hosting what is present, much like a guest house opening its doors

My approach combines systemic and narrative coaching, depending on what is being explored. The aim is not only to understand your situation, but to help you relate to it differently. With more clarity, spaciousness and compassion.

Whatever comes, a pattern, a loyalty, a fear or a story: it is welcomed and seen. What is hosted rather than resisted can settle and find its rightful place.

Together we explore what you come from, the systems that shape you, and the loyalties that influence your patterns. Through acknowledging what is, fully and without judgement, space opens: to breathe, to disentangle what has become knotted, to restore what you carry in a new way.

This is where movement becomes possible: not forced change, but change that grows from the foundation you already stand on.

Working together

Individual coaching for people navigating questions, stuckness, or a desire for meaningful change. Personal or professional.

Through an organisation

A trajectory of five sessions is recommended: enough space to move beyond the initial question and into the deeper patterns that sustain it.

As an individual

The structure is flexible. Some clients work with a long-term theme; others bring a different question to each session. We follow what emerges.

Format
  • Sessions of 60 to 90 minutes
  • In person, online or by phone
  • Typically every 3 to 4 weeks, allowing reflection and integration
